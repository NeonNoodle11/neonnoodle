About Neon Noodle

Neon Noodle is a unique outsider pop art project exploring Taured, neo-noir, and dark humor themes in short and long films and soundscapes. Dive into bizarre parallel universes and strange metaphysical tales with a retro yet eerie vibe. This project is gaining traction on YouTube.
